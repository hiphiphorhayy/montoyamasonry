About Us

When I came to the United States over 20 years ago, I sometimes worked 3 jobs just to cover all my expenses. When I started a family I knew I’d have to get into a new line of work to provide for my family. So I started working in construction. At first it was hard, but I learned very quickly. Before I knew it I was skilled enough to be able to replace my own boss. So I did.  I started Manuel Montoya Masonry Contractor LLC in 2001. My boss taught me how to craft professional grade works of art, so I specialize in masonry construction. With us you can rest assured that you will receive quality work. As a family-owned business, we understand the value of hard work and integrity. We treat every project as if it were our own. We decided to rebrand although we are always referred to by our clients. But we also thought it was time to get with the times. So we built this website to showcase our work for anyone who’s interested in improving their home.
